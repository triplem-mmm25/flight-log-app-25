Help & tips

Getting the most out of Travelers Log

Short answers to the things people ask most. Still stuck? Use Contact / feedback in the app footer.

Adding a flight

Tap + Add flight to type one in. Inside that window you can also choose Upload photos/files to add flights automatically from boarding-pass photos or PDFs.

Uploading boarding passes

Open + Add flight → Upload photos/files, pick one or more images or PDFs, and the flights are read out for you to confirm before saving.

Importing from another app

Open the Data menu → Import CSV. It works with exports from Flighty, MyFlightradar24 and App in the Air; the date, from, to and airline columns are matched automatically.

Places you visited before logging

Been somewhere before you started tracking flights? Scroll to Countries visited and add it there. It counts toward your totals and continents without needing a flight.

Goals and bucket list

From the dashboard you can set a country goal and a continents goal, and keep a bucket list of places you want to visit. Wishlist countries show in gold on the map.

Sharing your map

Tap Share to publish a read-only public link, then post it to WhatsApp, X or Facebook, or copy it. Turn it off any time and the link stops working.

Saving a PDF

Open the Data menu → Save PDF for a clean, printable copy of your dashboard.

Signing in on another device

Your flights and settings follow you. Sign in with the same email and password anywhere and everything is there.

Password & security

Change your password in Settings. You will need your current password, and the new one needs at least 8 characters with a capital letter, a lowercase letter and a number.
