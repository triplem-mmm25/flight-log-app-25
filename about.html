About us

Every flight you have taken, in one place.

Travelers Log turns your flight history into a living map. Log where you have been, see it drawn across the world, and watch the numbers behind your travels add up.

What it is

Travelers Log is a personal home for your flight history. Add a trip in seconds by hand, snap a boarding pass, or import a CSV, and it becomes an interactive world map, a running count of countries and continents, and a set of personal records and yearly recaps.

It is built for people who love looking back on where they have been, and looking forward to where they are going next.

What you can do

Map your worldAn interactive map of every route you have flown, plus a spinnable 3D globe and a shaded map of the countries you have reached.
Log flights three waysType them in, upload boarding-pass photos or PDFs that are read for you, or import a CSV from another tracker.
Count everythingCountries, continents, distance, time in the air, and personal records like your longest and shortest flights.
See patternsA monthly travel heatmap, growth over time, the days and months you fly most, and your connections and layovers.
Set goalsPick a country goal and a continents goal, keep a bucket list, and add places you visited before you started logging.
Look back and shareA Year in Review card, and an optional read-only public map you can share to WhatsApp, X or Facebook.

Your data is yours

Everything is private to your account and synced across your devices when you sign in. Nothing is ever sold, and nothing is shared unless you choose to publish a read-only map. You can turn that off at any time.
